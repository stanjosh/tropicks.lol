MENUS AND THEMING

WOMEN + MADNESS takes deep pride in our ability to create custom themed and non-themed menus in a fast and efficient manner to help boost your liquor sales, repeat business, and invite in new clientele. In addition, we offer bar education to help interested staff to not only personally benefit from trainings, but also to create their own tailored inspired menus. (because cross training is the future). If you teach a man to fish… you know the saying. Feel confident that we will supply you with the tools to maintain and educate so that you may continue creating a wonderful space, staff and menu. We make it a promise to always be available to you if needed, but you should never feel pigeon-holed into being fully reliant on anyone.

We also believe that the second-best part of menu creation (first, being the amazing drink you just created) is NAMING! It be ethereal, crass, punny, elegant or anything you want that epic name to be drink and we can show you some tips and tricks in doing so. We will also help show you the no-no’s to drink naming as well. Nobody wants to be cancelled or unintentionally offend.
